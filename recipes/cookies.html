Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C)
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt; gradually blend into the creamed mixture
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until edges are nicely browned
  8. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
